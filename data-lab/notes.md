## 浮点数的表示分为三类

以32位为例

![](.\img\Floating-Point Representation.png)

### $浮点数值V = (-1)^s \times M \times2^E$

### 规格化数(Normalized)

阶码范围e为1(00000001) ~ 254(11111110)

$E = e - bias$

$E_{min} = -126 \quad E_{max} = 127$

$M = 1 + f = 1.f_{22}f_{21}...f_{1}f_{0}$

### 非规格化数(Denormalized)

$E = 1 - bias$

$M = f$

### 特殊值

分为无穷大和负无穷大(Infinity)，以及NaN

Infinity:

$s = 0 \quad f = 0 \quad V = +∞$

$s = 1 \quad f = 0 \quad V = -∞$
